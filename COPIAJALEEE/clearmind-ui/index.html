What is ClearMind Logic

ClearMind Logic is a validation protocol designed to determine whether a decision should be executed.

It evaluates each decision considering:

  • Declared risk level
  • Current financial exposure
  • Available liquidity
  • Cash flow commitments
  • Strategic coherence

It produces a determination:

AUTHORIZED NOT AUTHORIZED

Without emotional bias.

Without impulsivity.

Without post-rationalization.

The decision remains outside the system.
So does the responsibility.

Final decision remains with the user. ClearMind Logic provides structured validation, not execution authority.
